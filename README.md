## Troubleshooting

If the cheat doesn't work:

- Ensure you ran the `.exe` **as Administrator**
- Check that your internet connection is stable
- Verify the `.minecraft` folder exists in `%appdata%`
- Temporarily disable your antivirus (Windows Defender, etc.)
